Portfolio


Hello, World! Welcome to my portfolio page. My name is Eric Summers, as I'm sure you've gathered. I currently reside east of Austin in a beautiful bend of the Lower Colorado River. I was born in Austin and have lived here quite happily most of my years. Although I have traveled a bit, and plan to travel more, Austin will always be my home. I have many interests as you'll surely discover. I hope you enjoy browsing my portfolio!
